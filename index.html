Thank You

I've been working on this universe, Iubris, for as long as I can remember. I still have scant memories of me as a little girl lying on rocks and looking up at the Icelandic sky turning the mythological stories of my ancestors into a world of my own. I use to imagine alternative stories to those my family told about the Æsir and Vanir; eventually making up my own version of Nordic mythology in my head. Around my early teens, I began to become really interested in fantasy books, especially with Tolkien and picked up a fascination with western esotericism and early Christianity. I combined these two newfound interests with my alterante mythology and that's where the world of Iubris really began.

In my late adolescence I became enamoured with classical literature and poetry and began to compose poems and write short stories based on events in Iubris. This collection of literature on Iubris has grown to a sizeable amount. MORE TO BE WRITTEN.
